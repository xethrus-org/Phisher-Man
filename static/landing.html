Phisher-Man: Failure Page

Why Are You Seeing This Page?

  In short, you're here because you failed a phishing test, one that was likely sent out by someone that you know. This phishing test was intended to collect statistics on how likely individuals are to fall for different types of phishing scams. Because you have failed this phishing test, a change in statistics has been sent to whoever created this phishing simulation.

What Does This Mean?

  This means that someone (likely an employer) now knows that you are vulnerable to a certain type of phishing scam. This can help the person who sent out this simulation to understand the best way to improve security among the individuals tested through this simulation. On an individual level, this means that you should likely improve upon how carefully you look at strange looking emails and train to be able to recognize a basic phishing email.
